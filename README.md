# FocusNest

FocusNest is a full-stack web-based study productivity platform developed to help students stay focused, organised, and consistent in their learning. It brings together time management, revision support, document-based AI assistance, and progress tracking in one connected workspace.

The platform was developed by **Aymen Laoufi** as a **Computer Science Final Year Project** at the **University of Westminster**.

## Features

- **Pomodoro Timer** with synced session state across pages
- **Forest system** that rewards completed study sessions with planted trees
- **Timetable** for planning structured study sessions
- **AI Assistant** with lightweight retrieval over uploaded study materials
- **Document upload** and text extraction for PDF, DOCX, and text files
- **Flashcard generation** from uploaded documents
- **Progress tracking** for study activity and session history
- **Profile management** with account settings and deletion flow
- **Dark mode** and **multilingual support**

## Tech Stack

- Next.js 16
- React 19
- TypeScript
- Tailwind CSS v4
- Firebase Authentication
- Firestore
- OpenAI
- Vercel

## Purpose

FocusNest was designed to address common student problems such as procrastination, fragmented study workflows, reduced concentration, and the need to switch between multiple separate tools. The goal is to provide a more integrated and focused digital study environment.
